Ban forms

Submit the form below to appeal your ban. It could take 1-2 days for us to review your appeal. While your appeal is being reviewed, do not remind staff to check your appeal. To check the status of your ban appeal, or to read other players' appeals, view the spreadsheet below. If your appeal is initially denied, you can re-appeal, but please wait 7 days before posting a new appeal.
